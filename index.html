About the lab

The Wireless Integrated Microsystems Lab locates at Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National Chiao-Tung University, Taiwan. Our research topics mainly ecompass the following areas:
1. Energy-efficient wireless communication circuits
2. Interface circuits for CMOS/MEMS sensors
3. Power management and energy harvesting techniques
4. RF/Mixed signal circuits and systems for biomedical applications
